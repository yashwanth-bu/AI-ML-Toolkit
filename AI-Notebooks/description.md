# AI Notebooks

This folder contains notes and study materials on **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**.  
It is meant to organize key concepts, research summaries, and technical references across various AI domains.

## Main Areas
- **Machine Learning:** Core algorithms, supervised and unsupervised methods.  
- **Deep Learning:** Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, and Transformers.  
- **Natural Language Processing:** Text understanding, embeddings, and language models.  
- **Computer Vision:** Image processing and visual recognition techniques.  
- **Reinforcement Learning:** Agent-environment interaction and reward optimization.  
- **AI Tools:** Frameworks like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and scikit-learn.  
- **Research Notes:** Paper summaries, insights, and references.  

## Purpose
To serve as a centralized, easy-to-update knowledge base for AI learning, experimentation, and research.
